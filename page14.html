" Bulakenyo cuisine has opulent flavors. Embracing our traditional food keeps our culture alive. In every Bulakenyo dish served in our tables, there is definitely a rich historical background about it. I grew up receiving Ensaymada as "pasalubong" from relatives who live in San Jose Del Monte. That ensaymada has the right amount of sweetness. The perfect combination of sugar and margarine gives the flavor uniqueness."

" When I was little, me and my family used to eat Pansit Alanganin after attending mass, so since then, that dish became my favorite. Bulakenyo food is an unorthodox traditional Filipino cuisine. From adding fresh milk to noodles, making sweet pork rinds, and more, the Bulakenyo taste is a twist to Filipino cuisine. It is a must to embrace Bulakenyo cuisine because for me, the food that a person eats reflects upon the person. Like how bikolenyos have spicy personalities, it is safe to say that we Bulakenyos are innovative."

Ron Louis Dela Cruz

" My all time favorite Bulacan delicacy is Pastillas- a well known local treat usually served after Filipino dishes. Pastillas are characterized by its sweet flavor- some have different variants like yam (ube), langka, cheese, macapuno etc. However, the standard pastillas of Metro Manila usually hardens as the dessert is being exposed to air.
In Bulacan, we also have a version of this dessert and I love how it remains soft despite the exposure; This is Pastillas de Leche. Leche is a spanish word which means milk... Since milk contains proteins and enzymes, it reacts with the components of the pastillas making it soft and a bit sweeter due to the presence of lactose."

Jose Marie Kyle Lazaro

" I love Inipit because the sweetness and the other ingredients it contains blend well. It also has various flavors which an individual can choose from.  Bulakenyo cuisine is innovative and delectable.  It is very significant to preserve our traditional food because we play a vital role in developing our province by supporting our traditional food and tourism. Through embracing the traditional food, we get to increase the growth of our economy and rise the entrepreneurship in Bulacan. Furthermore, food and native delicacies in this province are one of the factors for the tourists to visit our place."

Keith Alyanna Delos Reyes
