Ensaymada

If there is one pasalubong that you shouldn't leave without when visiting Malolos, Bulacan, that would be their yummy Ensaymada.  Many people say that you haven't eaten the best ensaymada until you've tasted Bulacan's. Ensaymada is a Filipino sweet bread, large snail-like coil dusted in sugar. They are soft, buttery, milky and rich, and with the added slather of butter, sugar, salted egg and cheese and Malolos, Bulacan is well-known in baking special ensaymadas in old-fashioned way.
